What is CBD?

CBD (Cannabidiol) is a naturally occurring compound found in hemp plants. Unlike THC, it is non-intoxicating and has been widely studied for its calming and anti-inflammatory effects.

Can you get 'high' from CBD?

No, CBD is non-psychoactive. It does not produce the 'high' commonly associated with marijuana, as it contains no or minimal THC content (typically under 0.3%).

Is CBD oil safe or addictive?

CBD is considered safe and non-addictive. Numerous studies and user experiences support its use for wellness, but it’s best to start with small doses and monitor your body's response.
